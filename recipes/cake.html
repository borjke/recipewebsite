Cake

A delicious chocolate cake, takes about 15-30 minutes to make

The recipe is not labor intensive at all, and only takes half an hour. You should expect a fluffy, sweet cake that will feed 4-6 people. It is like a normal cake, not like a cheesecake. It is best served with ice cream.

Ingredients Required

  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Salt
  • Sprinkles

Steps

  1. Mix eggs, milk, and flour along with sugar in a bowl
  2. Stir vigourosly for 2 minutes
  3. Sprinkle some salt
  4. Add Cocoa powder, and continue mixing
  5. Pour mixture into a cake tray, and place in over for 30 minutes at 425F
  6. Take out cake, flip tray and serve
  7. Put on sprinkles
