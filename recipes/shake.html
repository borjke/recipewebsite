Cake

A delicious strawberry shake, takes 5 min to make

The recipe is not labor intensive at all, and only takes af few minutes. You should expect a sweet, creamy shake that will be sure to satisfy you.

Ingredients Required

  • Strawberries
  • Milk
  • Ice
  • Sugar
  • Bananas
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix milk, strawberries, ice, sugar, bananas, and salt in a blender
  2. Turn on blender to high, and blend for 1 min
  3. Serve and enjoy
